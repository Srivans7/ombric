Diversity and Bioprospecting Analysis of Odisha's Coastal and Marine Flora

Institution: IISER Berhampur

Department: Department of Biological Sciences & Department of Chemical Sciences

Coordinator: Dr. Jose Sebastian

Investigators

  • Dr. Thirupathi Barla
  • Dr. Vinay Bulusu

IISER Berhampur

Objectives

  • Comprehensive profiling of novel bioactive compounds and gene discovery including chemical profiling, compound isolation, structural elucidation, functional characterisation, and gene annotation
  • Chemical engineering approaches towards product optimization and development of value-added products

Key Deliverables

  • Isolation and characterization of novel bioactive metabolites with scientific and commercial applications and development of upscaling technology
  • Identification of unique genes and alleles with commercial applications in pharmaceutics, nutraceuticals, agriculture, and eco-friendly pesticides
  • Chemical engineering approaches for development of high-value and value-added products from marine sources
  • Publications and patents for novel molecules and product development pathways
