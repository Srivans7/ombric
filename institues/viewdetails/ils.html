ILS Bhubaneswar

Objectives

  • Establishment of a state-of-the-art biorepository for cataloguing marine micro and macro organisms of Odisha
  • Generation of Odisha's marine bioresource atlas using genomics tools
  • Fostering marine biotechnology research and facilitating startup activities in Odisha

Key Deliverables

  • 1000 marine bacterial genome sequencing from Odisha coast
  • Development and optimisation of protocols for metagenomic and functional metagenomic studies
  • Establishment of a state-of-the-art biorepository for marine organisms
  • Generation of Odisha's Marine Bioresource Atlas using genomics tools
  • Promotion of marine biotechnology R&D and startup activities through ILS-Bio-Incubator
