Objectives

  • Biodiversity mapping of micro and macro algal communities, seaweeds, and establishment of marine cyanobacteria germplasm repository
  • Primary screening and characterization of bioactive metabolites from cyanobacteria and microalgae
  • Biodiversity mapping of marine fishes, isopods, amphipods, and zooplankton of Odisha coast and their translational applications

Key Deliverables

  • Biodiversity mapping and preparation of Odisha Marine Bio-Resource Atlas
  • Development of marine cyanobacteria and algae culture collection centre
  • Value addition and upscaling of bioactive metabolites such as phycocyanin, phycoerythrin, β-carotene, astaxanthin, and fatty acids
  • Value addition and product formulation from faunal samples
  • Scientific documentation of underwater ecosystems through SCUBA diving
  • Establishment of a state-of-the-art museum for fish and invertebrates
