Coastal Odisha Systematics Mapping, Monitoring and Observations for Sustainable Development

Institution: Indian Institute of Technology Bhubaneswar

Department: School of Earth, Ocean & Climate Sciences

Coordinator: Dr. Syed Hilal Farooq

Objectives

  • Comprehensive mapping and monitoring of Odisha's coastal marine bioresources
  • Assessment of ecosystem health at key bioreserve hotspots along Odisha's coastline
  • Skill development, knowledge sharing, and capacity building

Key Deliverables

  • Digitally mapped inventory of Odisha's coastal and submerged bioresources within 5 km on either side of the coastline
  • Interactive high-resolution maps for planners, researchers, and conservation agencies
  • Baseline dataset on coastal biodiversity including hotspot identification and carbon sequestration potential
  • Standard sampling protocols and user-friendly instruments for underwater sediment and water sample collection
  • Documentation of submerged bioresources through underwater photography and expert identification
  • Policy-ready report on ecosystem health and climate mitigation potential of Odisha's coast
  • Workshops, hands-on training modules, and technical support for capacity building and policy formulation
